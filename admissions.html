Admission Process

1

Application Submission

Complete and submit the online application form along with required documents.

Appear for the entrance test in core subjects based on the applying grade level.

  • English
  • Mathematics
  • Science (For Grade 6+)

Successful candidates will be called for an interview followed by enrollment.

  • Document Verification
