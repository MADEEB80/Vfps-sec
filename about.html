About VPSC

Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders Today

Our Mission

To provide quality education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, promotes academic excellence, and develops well-rounded individuals who are prepared to meet the challenges of tomorrow. We strive to create an environment that fosters creativity, critical thinking, and character development.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading educational institution that sets new standards in academic excellence and holistic development. We envision our students as future leaders who will make positive contributions to society through their knowledge, skills, and values.

Our Journey

1995

Establishment of VPSC with a vision to provide quality education

2000

Expansion to include secondary education

2005

Introduction of O/A Level programs

2020

Modernization of facilities and digital integration
